Why Career Councelling?

No one can discover you until you do. Exploit your talents, skills and strengths and make the world sit up and take notice.

~Rob Liang

Career counseling is important because it helps people make better choices about their education and jobs. It guides them to understand their skills, interests, and strengths, so they can choose a career that suits them. With so many career options, it can be confusing, but counseling makes it easier to decide. It also helps set realistic goals and gives advice on how to achieve them. By reducing stress and giving a clear plan, career counseling helps people feel more confident about their future. It also introduces new career options, helps build the right skills, and prepares people for changes in the job market. Overall, career counseling helps avoid choosing the wrong path, supports personal growth, and leads to a happy and successful future.
